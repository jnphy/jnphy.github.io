What is Project Oomfies?

Project Oomfies is a creative effort led by Trinidadian filmmaker, Jayson Jaskeran, and it aims to produce and expose rich stories from the minds of Caribbean filmmakers. Genres range from comedies and horrors to dramas and documentaries. The first film created under this proiect was Breakout Room with much more content on the way. Interested in working with us to help bring your project to fruition? Contact us below.
